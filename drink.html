Healthy Drinks

Green Tea:has been hailed for its health benefits in Asian countries for centuries; only recently did Americans get the memo.

Pomegranate Juice:All-natural pomegranate juice is one of the most nutritious beverages on supermarket shelves.

Beet Juice:Beets’ health benefits are abundant, starting with the fact that they’re a powerful source of folic acid, magnesium and nitrates.

Lemon Water:Lemon water is a surprisingly powerful immune booster. It contains vitamin C.

Kombucha:This elixir is one of the hottest healthy drinks on the market. Wellness junkies rave about kombucha’s health benefits.

Coconut Water :this healthy drink can hydrate the body and provide a surprising amount of potassium.IT regulates BP.

Hot Chocolate:this antioxidant-rich drink is even more beneficial when mixed with milk, which is high in calcium and usually fortified with vitamin D.

Cranberry Juice:This tangy juice is packed with antioxidants that can help prevent cardiovascular disease and some types of cancer.

Red Wine :Studies suggest that moderate amounts of any alcohol can help increase HDL (good cholesterol) and prevent artery damage.
