"physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness....."

Health can be defined as a state of complete physical, mental, social and spiritual well being, which consists in maintaining the body as much as possible by following daily advice and preventive measures to reduce the possibility of diseases.

Health has an important role in feeling happy, as there are many people who suffer from diseases, but the health enables them to fight diseases, as a result, they achieve physical fitness and feel comfortable or enjoy life.


Healthy Drinks

Green Tea:has been hailed for its health benefits in Asian countries for centuries; only recently did Americans get the memo.

Pomegranate Juice:All-natural pomegranate juice is one of the most nutritious beverages on supermarket shelves.

Beet Juice:Beets’ health benefits are abundant, starting with the fact that they’re a powerful source of folic acid, magnesium and nitrates.


Healthy Foods

Almonds:Almonds contain lots of healthy fats, fiber, protein, magnesium and vitamin E. The health benefits of almonds include lower blood sugar levels, reduced blood pressure and lower cholesterol levels.

Lentils:It can lower cholesterol and protect against diabetes and colon cancer. A daily dose of fiber pushes waste through your digestive system and prevents constipation, too.

Wheat germ:It's a great source of vegetable proteins, along with fiber and healthy fats. It's also a good source of magnesium, zinc, thiamin, folate, potassium, and phosphorus.


Yoga Asanas

Katichakrasana:Katichakrasana is good for relieving constipation as this yoga strengthens and improves the flexibility of the spine and waist.

Bhujangasana:Stimulates the heart and organs in the abdomen, like the kidneys. This yoga also relieves stress and fatigue.

Kurmasana:It stretches legs, back, shoulders and chest . Kurmasana improves the functions of the respiratory systems.
